Our Story.

Our company had a vision of revolutionizing package delivery. Our CEO actually tweeted at a few companies he thought could help us. One of these tweets was to Elon Musk. He talked with Elon for a while and pitched him our idea to use satellites and drop pods to send packages to anywhere including the ISS. Hearing his pitch Elon said SpaceX would like to collaborate and help us get our satellites in orbit.

The initial protoype tests of our products were damaging property and hurting people. We have made a lot of changes to the drop pods to stop this from happening as often.
